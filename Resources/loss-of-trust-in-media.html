Why people have lost polls in the media

People have lost trust in the media after many scandals, bias, and false reporting. Many people have realized that the media, even many outlets that claim to be “unbiased,” seem to push a narrative. This is a major issue in today’s society because people do not know what is true and what is false. One major recent scandal was the cover-up of the media of President Joe Biden’s mental decline, which may have done a number on the credibility of the media recently. There is also a great deal of political misinformation out there on places like X, which now do not censor anything (for some reason), and when people cannot trust the media, you have no idea what is true and what is false. That is the reason why we need unbiased news. With the recent election of Donald Trump, some of this has gotten even crazier, with the respective “party presses” racing to defend or attack him…”
